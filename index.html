The why

I lost this fight for years. Willpower never held.

I built this for people drowning in porn and the endless feed — people like me. The companies behind these apps are worth billions. You don't out-discipline a billion dollars.

I tried it all. Thousands of videos. Every blocker, every "system," every fresh Monday. Each time I caved — usually around 2am, alone, telling myself just this once.

So I stopped trusting the version of me that breaks. I built a lock that holds the line when I can't. No off switch in the exact moment you want one.

80% built. We finish the rest together.
